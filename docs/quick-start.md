# Quick Start Guide

Get up and running with the RANZ Quality Program portal in 5 minutes.

---

## 1. Log In

Navigate to **portal.ranz.org.nz** and sign in with your RANZ credentials. If you do not have an account, click **Sign Up** and follow the registration prompts.

If you are already signed in to another RANZ application (e.g., reports.ranz.org.nz), you will be authenticated automatically via SSO — no additional sign-in required.

---

## 2. Complete Onboarding

First-time users are directed to the **Set Up Your Business** page (`/onboarding`). Enter your business details:

| Field | Required | Notes |
|-------|----------|-------|
| Business Name | Yes | Legal business name (e.g., "Example Roofing Ltd") |
| Trading Name | No | If different from the business name |
| NZBN | No | 13-digit New Zealand Business Number |
| Business Email | Yes | Primary contact email for your business |
| Business Phone | No | Contact phone number |

Click **Create Business** to set up your organization. You are redirected to the dashboard.

---

## 3. Explore the Dashboard

The **Dashboard** (`/dashboard`) is your home page. It shows:

- **Stats Cards** — Insurance policies count, staff count, document count, and compliance score at a glance
- **Dimension Indicators** — Your scores across the 4 compliance dimensions (Documentation, Insurance, Personnel, Audit)
- **Compliance Score** — Your overall score and current certification tier (Accredited, Certified, or Master Roofer)
- **Expiring Insurance** — Policies expiring within 90 days, with days remaining and direct links
- **Action Items** — Critical and warning issues from the compliance engine, with links to resolve them

---

## 4. Key First Steps

| Step | Where | What to Do |
|------|-------|------------|
| Upload insurance | **Insurance** (`/insurance`) | Add your Public Liability, Professional Indemnity, and Statutory Liability policies |
| Add staff | **Staff** (`/staff`) | Add team members with LBP numbers for verification |
| Upload quality documents | **Documents** (`/documents`) | Upload documents against the 19 ISO quality elements |
| Add suppliers | **Suppliers** (`/suppliers`) | Register your approved suppliers |

---

## 5. Tips

- Use the **sidebar** to navigate between sections — Dashboard, Insurance, Staff, Documents, Suppliers, Projects, Audits, CAPA, and Settings
- Your **compliance score** updates automatically as you add insurance, staff, and documents
- **Expiry alerts** are sent via email (and SMS if enabled) at 90, 60, and 30 days before insurance policies expire
- The portal works on mobile browsers — bookmark **portal.ranz.org.nz** on your phone for on-the-go access
